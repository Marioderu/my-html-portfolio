The Best Movies According to Mario

My top 3 movies. 2023 Edition

The Super Mario Bros. Movie

It's my most favourite videogamer ever. I used to play it all the time and it's got my name on it! I loved the movie and all its references.

Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. 3

One of my favourite sagas from the MCU (Marvel Universe). James Gunn did a brilliant job, best movie of this saga.

Spider-man: Across the Spiderverse

I love the animation movies and I loved the first movie. This one is one of the best Spider-man movies ever made. It's got a lot of references of the comics, for those who read them.
